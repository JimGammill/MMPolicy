The Committee shall employ a Superintendent/Director and fix his/her compensation.  The Superintendent/Director shall act in accordance with General Laws, Chapter 71, Section 59, and shall perform such other duties consistent with this section as the Committee may determine.  He/She shall also prepare such reports as may be required by authorities having jurisdiction over the school district, such as the State and Federal Departments of Education, and shall submit materials for the Committeeâ€™s annual report to the Selection of member towns in sufficient time for printing in the annual report of the member municipalities.

**NOTE:  Under the laws of the Commonwealth, the School Committee may award a contract to a Superintendent/Director for a period not to exceed six years.**
